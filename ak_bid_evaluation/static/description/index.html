Bid Evaluation

Evaluate and Score Bids



Extend your bids review and selection process by evaluating bids (quotations) within Odoo. Build multiple checklists and evaluation/scoring questions and use depending on the type of the product or service being procured. Create purchase committees and invite users to take part in the evaluation process.

The app has been tested on both the Community and Enterprise versions.





Screenshots

Specify where to enable bid evaluation

You can specify whether to to enable bid evaluation for each purchase agreement type. Bid evaluations can only be enabled for competitive bids and not for blanket orders.



Setup evaluation templates

Maintain multiple evaluation templates and specify a template for each agreement. Customize your bids checklist and evaluation questions, along with the maximum score.



Conduct the evaluations



Request Receipt Acknowledgement Email

Vendors are automatically sent a receipt acknowledgement email notifying them of next steps and providing them with a reference number for their request.



Review and approve payment requests

Once submitted by vendors, Payment requests go through a review and approval process. A payment request is reviewed by a purchase representative followed by the approval of a Finance representative. Once approved, the payment is made and the vendor is notified via email.



Kanban Dashboard

A handy kanban dashboard will make it easy to access payment requests at different stages, as well as provide an overview of the number of requests per stage.



Email Notifications and Activities

Vendors are notified through email of when their requests are recieved and when processed. In addition, activities are automatically scheduled for relevant department users throughout the request's workflow.
